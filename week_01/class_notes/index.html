Buttermilk Pancakes

This is a description of this amazing recipe you should totally cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Optional Spices
    • cinnamon
    • nutmeg
    • salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. melt butter in pan
  3. spoon half cup batter into pan
  4. flip when golden
