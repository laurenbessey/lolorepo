WHAT?

The Book It! Zine Fair is a zine/artist book fair held by the Spring 2024 Critical Creative Production Approaches Seminar: Book It! class taught by Professor Susanna Horng. The event will feature the distribution of zines made by students of the class, a zine making workshop and exchange, food and refreshments, and guest speakers, including Global Liberal Studies alumni, Annie LeFevre.

Co-sponsored by Art, Text and Media, Cultural and Social Identities, Critical Creative Production, and Liberal Studies’ Writing departments, the event invites all Liberal Studies students to attend!


AND WHAT IS THIS BOOKSHELF..?

This bookshelf website acts as a digital archive for the zines that were distributed by the Book It! class students at the fair. It also aims to make the zines accessible to those who could not attend the fair, and aims to display the books with detail about their content and respective artists. Artist contact details have been provided for those wanting to learn more or get a copy of the zines themselves!
