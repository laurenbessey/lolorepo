Tap on each of the zine covers below to learn more!

Love In The City

By Lauren Bessey

A photo-zine about love in New York City, shot on the weeks leading up to Valentine’s Day 2024.

Photos Papers Patterns

By Lauren Bessey

The name is self-explanatory! Photos Papers Patterns is a collage zine with all the photos, papers and patterns you could ever ask for.

Twentysix Transport Tickets

By Lauren Bessey

A riff off of Ed Ruscha’s ‘Twentysix Gasoline Stations’, this zine displays twenty-six transport tickets and cards from around the world.

My Ring Collection

By Lauren Bessey

A hand drawn collection with thoughts and memories about each of Lauren’s rings that she wears daily.

My Mum Thinks I Hate Men

By Lauren Bessey

A zine about feminism and queerness. Collaged with colourful paper, stickers, thoughts and feelings about a girl’s relationship with her mother.

Tradition, Context, Place and Time in Art

By Lauren Bessey

A one-sheet zine about how tradition, context, place, and time affects the viewing and reception of art across the world.

A Girl's Story

By Soleil Protos

A black and white story of a young girl.

Be Mine

By Sofie Hatto

A Valentine's Day note to a Valentine's Day lover. Be mine?

Cigarettes & Hotdogs

By Diana Andrade

A photo book exploring a girl's relationship with cigarettes, hotdogs, and her best friend.

Calendar

By Lauren Bessey

Calendar lets you see everyday of the year, all in one book. Wow!

Dream a Little Dream of Me

By Ares Maia

This zine is about feeling loss of identity, longing for belonging and trying to find a safe space.

A World of Her Own

By Kumi Korf and Catherine Alice Michaelis

This catalog opens with an essay by the curator of the exhibition Catherine Alice Michaelis and follows with an illustrated checklist of artworks show at Center for Book Arts during Korf’s exhibition at Center for Book Arts between July 14 and September 9, 2023.
