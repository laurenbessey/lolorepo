ZINE ???

A zine is a self-published magazine, usually both small themselves and in circulation, and often reproduced by a copy machine. Zines often consist of text, images, artwork, and other creative material, and are usually DIY-esque. They often are used as a medium of communication in sub-cultures and can help people to express their identity and form community, however a zine can really be whatever you want it to be! Zines are also usually inexpensive or free, like the ones in this bookshelf. We love zines!
